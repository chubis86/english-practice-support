Privacy Policy

English Practice Daily — Last updated July 23, 2026

This policy explains what information English Practice Daily collects, why, and how it's used. We collect the minimum data needed to run the app and never sell your information or use it for advertising.

Information we collect

DataWhy we collect it
Email addressTo create and log into your account
Account identifierTo associate your progress and purchases with your account
Purchase historyTo unlock the modules you've bought and restore purchases on new devices
Audio recordings (Speaking Practice exercises)To generate AI-powered pronunciation feedback
Push notification tokenTo send you optional practice reminders

How your data is used

All data listed above is used solely to make the app work: authenticating you, unlocking content you've paid for, and generating feedback on your speaking exercises. We do not use your data for advertising, and we do not sell or share it with data brokers.

Third-party services

We rely on a small number of service providers to run the app:

  • Supabase — hosts our database, authentication, and file storage.
  • RevenueCat — processes in-app purchases and subscription status. Payment itself is handled by the Apple App Store or Google Play, not by us directly.
  • Anthropic (Claude API) — analyzes your speaking exercise audio to generate feedback.

These providers only receive the data necessary to perform their specific function and are not permitted to use it for their own purposes.

Data retention and deletion

Your data is kept as long as your account is active. You can request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by emailing us — see Contact below.

Children's privacy

English Practice Daily is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, we'll update the date at the top of this page.
