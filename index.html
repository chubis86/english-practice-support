Common questions

  • I purchased a module but it's not unlocking. Try closing and reopening the app. If the module still doesn't unlock, email us with the email address you used to sign up.
  • I want to restore a purchase on a new device. Log in with the same account you used originally — purchases are tied to your account, not the device.
  • How do I request a refund? Refunds are handled directly by the App Store or Google Play, depending on where you purchased. You can request one from your purchase history in either store.
  • I found a bug or the audio isn't playing correctly. Email us with your device model and a description of what happened — screenshots help a lot.
